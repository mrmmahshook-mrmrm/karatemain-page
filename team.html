Chief Instructor

Sensei Abdu Rahiman K

— 4th Dan Black Belt · Chief Instructor & Examiner

With over 20 years of relentless dedication to the martial arts, Sensei Rahiman serves as Malappuram District Chief of the Nihon Shotokan Karate Association and as an A-Grade Karate Judge and Referee for the Kerala State.

His pedagogy weaves Karate, Kick Boxing, Muay Boran, Kalari, and Okinawa Kobudo into a singular path that produces both champions and gentlemen.
