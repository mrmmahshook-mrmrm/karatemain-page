Our Sensei
THE SENSEI THE LEGEND

Sensei Abdu Rahiman K is a passionate martial arts instructor from Malappuram, Kerala, with over 20 years of karate experience. He holds a prestigious Fourth Dan Black Belt, attested by Grand Master Kancho Masaya Kohama of I.S.S.K, reflecting his lifelong dedication to the art.

With 15 years of teaching experience, Sensei Rahiman serves as the Nihon Shotokan Karate Association Malappuram District Chief Instructor and an A-Grade Karate Judge and Referee in Kerala State. He has trained under world-class masters across multiple disciplines including Kick Boxing, Muay Boran, Kalari, and Okinawa Kobudo.

Beyond the dojo, Sensei Rahiman is a warm and inspiring individual fluent in English, Malayalam, and Arabic. His mission is simple — to build not just skilled fighters, but disciplined, confident, and well-rounded human beings through the transformative power of martial arts.
